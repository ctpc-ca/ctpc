# #Launch – Canadian Teen Programming Competition (CTPC)

## About

#Launch is a national student-led programming competition dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators, problem-solvers, and technology leaders. Founded by students, the competition provides opportunities for participants to develop technical skills, connect with industry professionals, and engage with a growing community of aspiring technologists.

## Mission

Our mission is to make technology education more accessible, engaging, and inclusive by creating meaningful opportunities for students to explore computer science, engineering, and innovation.

## What We Do

- Host annual programming competitions for students across Canada
- Connect participants with industry professionals and mentors
- Organize workshops, networking events, and educational programming
- Foster a supportive community of young innovators
- Provide opportunities for students to develop both technical and professional skills

## Impact

- 800+ participants engaged across Canada
- Partnerships with leading organizations and industry sponsors
- Thousands of dollars in sponsorship support secured for student initiatives
- A growing network of volunteers, mentors, educators, and student leaders

## Core Values

### Accessibility

Technology opportunities should be available to students regardless of background or experience level.

### Innovation

We encourage creativity, curiosity, and problem-solving through hands-on learning experiences.

### Community

We believe meaningful connections and collaboration are essential for personal and professional growth.

### Growth

We strive to help students build confidence, develop new skills, and discover future opportunities in technology.
